OneMuslim is designed to provide prayer times, Qibla direction, Adhan notifications, and related Islamic utilities while respecting user privacy.

OneMuslim may use your device location to calculate prayer times and Qibla direction. Location data is processed on your device and is not sold or shared.

OneMuslim may request notification permission to remind you of prayer times and play optional Adhan audio.
